# Superforecasting: The Art and Science of Prediction

**Book by Philip E. Tetlock and Dan Gardner**

[Goodreads Link](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/23995360-superforecasting)

## Why This Book?

Tetlock famously proved that expert forecasters perform no better than dart-throwing chimps. But here's the twist: a rare 2% consistently beat the odds. What's their secret? **It's learnable.**

Often called the sequel to Kahneman's *Thinking, Fast and Slow*.
