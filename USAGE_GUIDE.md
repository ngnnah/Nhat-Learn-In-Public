## ğŸ“ Content Guidelines

### What to Include

For each topic category, consider documenting:

- **What I Learned**: New concepts, facts, or insights
- **Actions/Practice**: What you actually did (projects, exercises, activities)
- **Resources**: Books, articles, videos, courses that helped you learn
- **Reflections**: Your thoughts, feelings, and insights about the learning

### Keep It Authentic

- Don't worry about being perfect - this is a learning journal, not a polished blog
- Be honest about challenges and failures
- Include both big and small learnings
- It's okay to skip topics where you didn't learn anything that week

### Make It Searchable

- Use clear, descriptive language
- Include specific names of tools, frameworks, or concepts
- Add links to resources when possible

**Q: What if I didn't learn anything in a category this week?**  
A: That's okay! You can leave sections blank or remove them. Not every week will cover all topics.

**Q: How detailed should I be?**  
A: Whatever works for you. Some weeks might have detailed notes, others might be brief bullet points.

**Q: Can I add my own topic categories?**  
A: Absolutely! This is your journal. Adapt it to fit your life and goals.

**Q: Should I include sensitive or personal information?**  
A: Remember this is a public repository. Only share what you're comfortable with others seeing.
